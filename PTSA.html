PTSA

Parent Teacher Student Association

What is PTSA

PTSA is a volunteer organization that brings together parents, teachers, students, and other community members to support the school and its students. PTAs work to improve the educational experience for all students by organizing events, fundraising, advocating for students' needs, and providing resources to teachers. They play a crucial role in creating a positive and supportive school environment.

Important Info

When you pay for a membership the money goes to supporting campus wide events, faculty/staff meals and student incentives. The point of PTSA is to build cooperation between faculty/staff, ctudents, and parents for a stronger community.
