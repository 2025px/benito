Starbooks Cafe Book Club

What is Starbooks Cafe Book Club?

Starbooks is a book club for students who enjoy a quiet, comfortable place to read and talk about books. Just bring a book and be ready to chill.

Important Info

The book club meets during lunches on November 7 and December 5 & 12.

Join us

To join Starbooks Cafe Book Club you have to sign up with the media center specialist (Mrs. Griner) by 11/1 to join.
