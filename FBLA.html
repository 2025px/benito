FBLA

Future Business Leaders of America

What is FBLA

FBLA is a career and technical student organization that prepares students for business careers through leadership development, competitive events, and community service. It's one of the largest student business organizations in the world, with members from middle school to college levels.

Important Info

There are general meetings on the first monday of each month. There are also various executive meetings each month for officers. There is the chance to apply to become a junor officer and help with all the FBLA activites, trips, and more.

Join us

To join the FBLA you need to ask to receive a packet from the Chapter Leader at the start of the school year. You must then fill out the forms and pay the dues to join and be a part of the FBLA.
