National Juior Honor Society

What is NJHS

NJHS is an organization that recognizes outstanding middle school students who excel in scholarship, leadership, service, character, and citizenship. Membership is a prestigious honor that signifies academic achievement and a commitment to community involvement.

Important Info

There are meetings every school quarter (9 weeks). In order to stay a member, you must retain good grades and conduct throughout the academic year. You must gain 25 hours of volunteer work and complete a service project with up to 2 partners.

Join us

To join NJHS, you need to receive an invitation in the second semester of 6th grade. After you are invited, you will be required to write an essay and receive an endorsement from a teacher.
