Welcome to Benito Middle School

Benito Middle School is the perfect place for students who are driven to succeed! Located within the Cross Creek community, a culture of being victorious is our way of life, as more than 90% of our students are routinely on the honor roll.

Academic Achievements: Benito Middle School consistently fosters a culture of academic excellence, where students are encouraged to reach their full potential. Our students excel on standardized tests, demonstrating strong performance in core subjects such as math, science, and language arts. Our dedicated teachers provide engaging instruction and support, preparing students for success in high school and beyond.

Athletic Achievements: Benito Middle School has a rich tradition of athletic success, with our teams competing at a high level in various sports. Our students demonstrate exceptional skill, teamwork, and sportsmanship, winning numerous championships in basketball, volleyball, soccer, and other athletic competitions. Our state-of-the-art athletic facilities provide an ideal environment for student-athletes to develop their skills and achieve their goals.

Vibrant Arts Programs: Benito Middle School offers a wide range of arts programs that nurture creativity and expression. Our talented students participate in band, choir, orchestra, and visual arts, showcasing their skills in competitions and performances. Our dedicated arts faculty provide high-quality instruction and support, inspiring students to explore their artistic passions and develop their talents.
