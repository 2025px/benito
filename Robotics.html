Robotics

What is the Robotics Club?

Robotics Club is a student-led organization that focuses on STEM education and robotics. Students learn about engineering, programming, and problem-solving skills with hands-on projects and engaging competitions. The club wants to invigorate creativity, teamwork, and a passion for technology among the students.

Important Info

There are meetings held in Robotics which take place every Tuesday during 7th grade lunch.

Join Us
