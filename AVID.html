AVID

Advancement Via Individual Determination

What is AVID

AVID is a college readiness program designed to help students develop the skills necessary for academic success. It focuses on critical thinking, writing, inquiry, collaboration, organization, and time management. AVID provides students with support and resources to help them reach their best and prepare for college and beyond.
